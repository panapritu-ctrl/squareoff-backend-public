Privacy Policy for SquareOff

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SquareOff. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our application.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data: includes your avatar gender and date of birth (for age verification).
  • Device Data: includes device identifiers, operating system, and hardware details used for security and anti-cheat purposes.
  • Audio/Camera Data: We access your microphone locally during live matchmaking to enable voice chat. While some components may request camera access for future video features, we do not record or store any audio or video data on our servers. All live communication is transmitted securely via Agora.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide the core gaming experience (matchmaking, leaderboards).
  • Enforce fair play and prevent cheating.
  • Serve personalized advertisements via Google AdMob (with your consent).

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use your device identifier to serve personalized ads if you have granted consent. You can manage your ad preferences at any time in the app settings or via your device settings.
